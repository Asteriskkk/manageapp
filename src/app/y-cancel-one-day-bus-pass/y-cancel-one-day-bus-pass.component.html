Here you can cancel your raised one day bus pass.

  • How to invoke Cancel One Day Bus Pass from Teams COCO?

  1. Click on CTM in main menu or from the command list, list of options will be sent to you by Teams COCO, find Cancel one day Bus Pass in the list and click on it. You will be able to see the list of your current one day bus pass details, click on the one you want to cancel.

  2. Simply type in “cancel one day bus pass” and You will be able to see the list of your current one day bus pass details, click on the one you want to cancel without much navigation.
