Here is where you change your passoword when it is about to expire.

  • How to invoke Change Password from Teams COCO?

  1. Click on A.R.T in main menu or from the command list, list of options will be sent to you by Teams COCO, find Change Password in the list and click on it. This will display a card with change password button, click on it and it will redirect you to change password url.

  2. Simply type in “Change Password” and this will display a card with change password button, click on it and it will redirect you to change password url.
