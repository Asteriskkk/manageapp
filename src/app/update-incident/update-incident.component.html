  • How to invoke Update Incident from Teams COCO?

  1. Click on AskGenie in main menu or from the command list, list of options will be sent to you by Teams COCO, find update incident in the list and click on it. This will display you a card with previous incidents, click on the incident that you want to update, fill in the comment and submit it.

  2. Simply type in “update incident” or “I want to update my incidents” and this will directly display the update incident card without any navigation.
