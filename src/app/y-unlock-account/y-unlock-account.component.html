In case your account has been locked out, this is where you come to unlock it.

  • How to invoke Unlock Account from Teams COCO?

  1. Click on A.R.T in main menu or from the command list, list of options will be sent to you by Teams COCO, find Unlock Account in the list and click on it. This will display a card asking in which way you want to unlock your account. Proceed by choosing your preferred option to successfully unclock your account.

  2. Simply type in “Unclock account” and This will display a card asking in which way you want to unlock your account. Proceed by choosing your preferred option to successfully unclock your account.
