• How many days of earned leave am I entitled for in a year?

  • All regular employees are entitled for 24 Earned Leaves in a calendar year. However, if the employee has joined the organization in the middle of the calendar year, then the quantum of Earned Leaves will be pro rata basis.

  • If I take 2 weeks of leave, will the intervening Saturday & Sunday get accounted for/deducted from my leave balance?

  • If you are applying for Earned Leave (EL), only the weekdays will be accounted for the leave debit and not the intervening Saturday and Sunday.

  • Can I get advance leave credit in my leave account?

  • Organization does not have any policy of advance leave.

  • How can I apply for LOP in the system?

  • Employees cannot apply for LOP in the system. Only the HRSD team can do the needful with required approvals.
