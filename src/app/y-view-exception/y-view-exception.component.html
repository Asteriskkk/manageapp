This is a place where you can check your raised exceptions with their statuses like whether they are pending or approved or rejected etc.

  • How to invoke View Exceptions from Teams COCO?

  1. Click on Attendance in main menu or from the command list, list of options will be sent to you by Teams COCO, find View Exceptions in the list and click on it. This will display you your Exceptions.

  2. Simply type in “View Exceptions” or “Show me Previous Exceptions” and this will directly display the View Exceptions without any navigation.
