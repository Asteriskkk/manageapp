Here you can view you raised cab requests for details like status etc.

  • How to invoke View Cab Requests from Teams COCO?

  1. Click on CTM in main menu or from the command list, list of options will be sent to you by Teams COCO, find View My Cab Requests in the list and click on it. You will be able to see the list of your previously raised cab requests

  2. Simply type in “view cab requests” and You will be able to see the list of your previously raised cab requests without much navigation.
