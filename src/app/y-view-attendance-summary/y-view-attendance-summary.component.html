This is a place where you can check your Attendance summary for a range of dates. It will show data like which days you have attended, taken leave, exception days and absence days.

  • How to invoke View Attendance Summary from COCO?

  1. Click on Attendance in main menu or from the command list, list of options will be sent to you by Teams COCO, find View Attendance Summary in the list and click on it. This will display you a card asking you to enter the range of dates you want to view your attendance summary for.

  2. Simply type in “View attendance summary” or “I want to see my attendance summary” and this will directly display the View attendance summary without any navigation.
