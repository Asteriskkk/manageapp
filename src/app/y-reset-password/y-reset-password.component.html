In Case you forgot your password and unable to recall it then use reset password functionality to reset it.

  • How to invoke Reset Password from Teams COCO?

  1. Click on A.R.T in main menu or from the command list, list of options will be sent to you by Teams COCO, find Reset Password in the list and click on it. This will First Authenticate you and then redirect you to reset your password.

  2. Simply type in “Reset Password” and This will First Authentication you and then redirect you to reset your password.
