Helps you raise STG or Non-STG incidents which you face in Hexaware. Incidents do not need any approval from your supervisor.

  • How to invoke Create Incident from Teams COCO?

  1. Click on AskGenie in main menu or from the command list, list of options will be sent to you by Teams COCO, find create incident in the list and click on it. This will display you a card wherein you should describe your incident in few words.

  2. Simply type in “create incident” or “I want to raise an incident” and this will directly display the create incident card without any navigation.
