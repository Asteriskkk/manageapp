Here you can cancel your raised cab requests.

  • How to invoke Cancel Cab Requests from Teams COCO?

  1. Click on CTM in main menu or from the command list, list of options will be sent to you by Teams COCO, find Cancel Cab Requests in the list and click on it. You will be able to see the list of your previously raised cab requests, click on the one that you want to cancel

  2. Simply type in “cancel cab requests” and You will be able to see the list of your previously raised cab requests, click on the one that you want to cancel without much navigation.
