Here you can raise your attendace related exceptions. Exceptions for working days should be raised under Attendance exception tab and exceptions for weekends/holidays should be raised under Holiday Exception Tab

  • How to invoke Raise Exception from Teams COCO?

  1. Click on Attendance in main menu or from the command list, find Raise Exception in the list and click on it. This will display you a card with pre populated data for yesterdays date. Change the data if you wish to raise for some other date or reason. Thereafter click on submit button to raise exception.

  2. Directly type in “Raise Exception” and you will get the raise exception card with pre populated data without any navigation.

  3. Utterances like “Raise exception for last Friday”, “Raise exception for yesterday” will bring up card with those dates.
