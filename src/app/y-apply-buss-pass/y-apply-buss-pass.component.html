You can apply your permanent bus travel pass here.

  • How to invoke Apply Bus Pass from Teams COCO?

  1. Click on CTM in main menu or from the command list, list of options will be sent to you by Teams COCO, find Apply bus Pass in the list and click on it. This will display a card with pre populated data, please change it as per your requirement and submit it to book the seat in the bus.

  2. Simply type in “Apply Bus Pass” and will display a card with pre populated data, please change it as per your requirement and submit it to book the seat in the bus without much navigation.
