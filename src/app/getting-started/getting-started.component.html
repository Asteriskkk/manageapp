• Getting Started With Teams COCO

  1. After adding Teams COCO bot to your chat list, you will have to login first. Invoke the login card by sending "signin" message to the bot.

  2. Please enter your Hexaware credentials to complete the login process.

  3. Once you are successfully logged in, you should be able to see a main menu screen. You are now ready to use Teams COCO for your self-service.
