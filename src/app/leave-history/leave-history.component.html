This is a place where you can check your applied leaves with their statuses like whether they are pending or approved or rejected etc.

  • How to invoke leave history from Teams COCO?

  1. Click on Absence in main menu or from the command list, list of options will be sent to you by Teams COCO, find Leave History in the list and click on it. This will display you leave History.

  2. Simply type in “Leave History” or “Show me my leave History” or “display Leave History” and this will directly display the leave history without any navigation.
