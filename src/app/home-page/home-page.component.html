Teams COCO is an always-on, 'in-context', persistent, omnichannel chat bot with a simple interface and intuitive user journey. Teams COCO allows employees to easily access information and undertake transactions across disparate IT systems, channels and business functions for HR, Payroll, Benefits, Travel, Expense, Admin etc.. It uses Microsoft LUIS NLP to capture intent and pass to and, extract transactions, from complex back end systems, thereby creating a seamless employee experience.
